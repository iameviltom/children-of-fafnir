a wholesome VR puzzle platformer with tiny dragons 🐉



Children of Fafnir is a wholesome VR puzzle platformer where you must rescue a bunch of tiny dragons.

  • 🐉  control tiny dragons
  • 🛠  play with physics objects
  • 🏝  adorable miniature floating islands
  • 🧩  simple but satisfying puzzles
  • 🔮  a mysterious ending
  • 💸  free!


The game is currently available as a 💸 free early access demo. This version includes 7 levels, representing the first area of the game and accounting for roughly ⏱ 45 minutes to 1 hour of gameplay.
