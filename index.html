a wholesome VR puzzle platformer with tiny dragons



Children of Fafnir is a wholesome VR puzzle platformer where you must rescue a bunch of tiny dragons. Directly control little dragons with the analogue sticks while using your hands to interact with and mainpulate physics objects in the world around you.


The game is currently available as a free early access demo. This version includes 7 levels, representing the first area of the game and accounting for roughly 45 minutes to 1 hour of gameplay.
